Payments, collections, and much more

What is EduEventor?

It's a very user-friendly system for group management, where you can add students, create fees for school or extracurricular activities, and view the history of all payments. For parents/guardians, the system enables quick online payments. You always know what you're paying for and how much!

Is it for you?

If you're doing many tasks manually, managing students, creating groups, and checking attendance will save you a lot of time! Also, if you're using complex and expensive systems that exceed your needs, you will love the simplicity and clarity of our solution.

Working with others?

If you're working with other teachers/instructors, the system simplifies logical management of various institutions. Your collaborators have access precisely and only to the organizations where they teach or conduct classes.

Features and Capabilities

What can EduEventor do for you?

The system will handle the boring tasks that you currently have to do yourself. See what EduEventor can do for you.

Lesson planning and scheduling

We provide tools for easy and efficient lesson planning, helping you organize time, manage priorities, and maintain balance in your daily schedule.

Student and group management

We offer comprehensive solutions for managing students and groups to help you effectively create a dynamic, interactive educational environment.

Receiving online payments

We enable convenient and secure online payments, giving you fast and smooth financial transactions. Accept multiple payment methods, monitor transactions, and offer a seamless experience to your clients.

Email* and SMS** communication

We provide effective communication tools via email and SMS, helping you easily connect with students/guardians and send important notifications, maintaining strong relationships.

Attendance tracking

We offer convenient and accurate attendance tracking, so you can effectively monitor participation and track attendance history.

Cash or bank transfer payments

We make it easy to manage payments, track transaction history, and give you peace of mind knowing your clients can pay in their preferred method.

Online sign-ups

You can create an online sign-up form where students will sign-up for your course/workshop/event. This gives you confidence the data provided is as accurate as possible.

Statistics: attendance, costs, income

Our app makes it easy to track and evaluate attendance levels, monitor expenses, and analyze income. Our advanced statistics features allow for detailed reporting, trend identification, and informed business decisions.

Free access for students and/or guardians

Our app offers free access for students/guardians, letting them use core features without fees. They can follow schedules, view payment history, and pay for classes online.

** Separate fees for SMS, outside of selected package, charged separately per usage
